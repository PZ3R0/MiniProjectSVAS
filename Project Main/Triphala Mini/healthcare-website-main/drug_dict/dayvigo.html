What is DayVigo?

If you have insomnia (trouble sleeping), your doctor may prescribe Dayvigo for you. Itâ€™s a prescription drug for certain adults who have trouble falling asleep or staying asleep.

DayVigo basics

The active ingredient in Dayvigo is lemborexant. (An active ingredient is what makes a drug work.) Dayvigo comes as a tablet that you swallow. It belongs to a group of drugs called orexin receptor antagonists. Dayvigo helps you sleep by blocking orexin (a brain chemical) from sending signals to your body to wake up.
